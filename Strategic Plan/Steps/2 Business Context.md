## 2. Business Context (What You Are Protecting)

**Goal:** Align security with how the company actually operates.

**Document**
- Business model (SaaS, retail, fintech, services, etc.)
- Critical assets:
  - Customer data
  - Revenue-generating systems
  - Intellectual property
- Regulatory drivers (if any): ISO, PCI, GDPR, local laws

**Output**
- Short business overview  
- List of “crown jewels”
